About Me

I am a composer for film & media, blending orchestral depth with modern textures to elevate emotion and storytelling on screen. I write with narrative in mind—music that breathes with picture, but also stands on its own when needed.

Vision

Music should act like its own character—supporting, revealing, and transforming scenes. My goal is to craft scores that leave a lasting emotional imprint.

Tools & Skills

  • Orchestration, arrangement, soundtrack production
  • Mixing & mastering for visual media
